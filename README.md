# 🌸 Petal Potions

**Petal Potions** is a VR Unity project where you play as a little fairy who plants seeds, grows flowers, and brews magical potions from their petals. Players can sell their potions to earn **Starshards**, which can then be used to buy new seeds and keep the cycle of gardening and crafting going.  

---

## 🎮 About the Project
This game was created as a **university project** by a two-person team:  
- **Lilli Ölsinger** – Programming  
- **Leonie Sattlecker** – 3D Modelling & UI Design  

Nearly all 3D assets (such as potions, flowers, and even the house interior) were custom-made, giving the game a unique and personal style.  

---

## ⚙️ Technical Details
- Built with **Unity XR Interaction Toolkit**  
- Tested on **Oculus Quest 3** and **Oculus Rift-S**  
- Features:  
  - Basic VR movement and interaction  
  - Visual & audio feedback for immersion  
  - Gardening and potion-brewing gameplay loop  

---

## ✨ Features
- Plant seeds & grow magical flowers  
- Brew potions from harvested petals  
- Sell potions to earn Starshards  
- Purchase new seeds to expand your garden
